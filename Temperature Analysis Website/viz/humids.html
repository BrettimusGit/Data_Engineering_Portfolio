Latitude vs Humidity

Hypothesis: Cities in the Southern Hemisphere (cities < 0° Latitude) will have higher humidity since there is more water in the Southern Hemisphere.

When plotting the city latitude vs. Humidity, no clear correlation can be seen. The graph above shows that cities in the Southern Hemisphere are just as likely to have a humidity between 5% and 100% as cities in the Northern Hemisphere. This proves that humidity can vary at any distance away from the equator (0° Latitude).
