Latitude vs Wind Speed

Hypothesis: Wind speed is related to weather and there is no correlation between a city’s distance from the equator (0° Latitude) and wind speed.

The graph confirms our hypothesis that there is no clear correlation between a city’s wind speed and their distance from the equator. The majority of cities in both the Southern and Northern Hemisphere have wind speeds below 20 mph. The Northern Hemisphere has a few more data points where cities wind speeds were > 20 mph, however; a city’s distance from the equator does not appear to influence total wind speed.

From the graph we can conclude that despite a city’s distance from the equator, wind speed will likely range between 0 and 35 mph on any given day.
